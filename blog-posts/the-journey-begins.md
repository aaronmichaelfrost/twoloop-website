## Our Origin Story *by Aaron*

Twoloop Games was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: create breathtakingly original multiplayer experiences that prioritize polish and replayability. As an independent, self-published studio, we have the freedom to pursue our creative vision without compromise.

## Our Team *by Rylan*

Our small but passionate team brings years of professional AAA experience to every project. We believe that great games come from great teamwork, attention to detail, and an unwavering commitment to player experience.

## PC-First Philosophy *by Aaron*

We're proud to be a PC-first studio. This allows us to:

- Take advantage of cutting-edge hardware capabilities
- Implement complex gameplay mechanics
- Provide high levels of customization
- Support modding communities
- Deliver the performance and visual fidelity PC gamers expect

## What Makes Us Different *by Rylan*

In an industry often focused on quick releases and maximum profit, we take a different approach:

- **Quality over Quantity**: We'd rather release one exceptional game than multiple mediocre ones
- **Community-Driven**: Player feedback shapes our development process
- **Innovation**: We're not afraid to experiment with new ideas and mechanics
- **Long-term Support**: Our games are built to last, with ongoing updates and community support

## Current Project: Fractium *by Aaron*

Our flagship project, Fractium, represents everything we stand for. It's a hardcore multiplayer experience that combines mathematical beauty with intense gameplay. Every aspect of the game has been carefully crafted to provide depth, challenge, and visual spectacle.

## Looking Ahead *by Aaron & Rylan*

This blog will be your window into our development process. Expect regular updates about:

- Development progress and behind-the-scenes insights
- Technical deep dives into our engine and tools
- Community highlights and player feedback
- Industry thoughts and reflections
- Sneak peeks at upcoming features

Thank you for joining us on this journey. The best is yet to come!
